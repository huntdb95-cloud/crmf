Meet Your Captain

Captain Dan

I grew up in Michigan but fell in love with the warm waters and wildlife of Florida at a young age. After spending countless trips exploring the Gulf Coast and the springs of Crystal River, I knew this was where I wanted to be.

I earned my captain’s license through Chapman’s Sea School and have spent most of my adult life in and around the springs here in Crystal River. Guiding guests to respectfully experience Florida’s gentle manatees is not just my job – it’s my passion.

On every tour, I focus on safety, education, and fun. Whether this is your first time in the water or you’re a seasoned snorkeler, I’ll make sure you feel comfortable, informed, and ready for an unforgettable adventure.

Come join me on the water – let’s make some manatee memories.
